Yonkett App - Website Plan

Site Name

Yonkett App

This name was selected because it represents a modern and dynamic platform for car enthusiasts. "Yonkett" suggests connection and community, which are the fundamental pillars of our application.

Available domain: yonkett.com

Site Purpose

The Yonkett App website will serve as:

  • Central hub for specific vehicle owners to create detailed car profiles
  • Community platform to connect owners of the same vehicle model
  • Maintenance and fuel expense tracking system
  • Future marketplace for buying/selling model-specific parts and accessories

Scenarios

Questions that target users would ask:

New User

"How can I find other owners of my same Toyota Corolla 2020 model to share experiences and maintenance tips?"

Advanced User

"Where can I register and track my fuel and maintenance expenses to have a complete history of my vehicle?"

Technical User

"Will the app allow me to manage multiple vehicles and receive personalized maintenance reminders for each one?"

Color Schema

The selected color palette reflects the energy and passion of the automotive world while maintaining professionalism:

Primary Red (#FA0001)

Used for: Headings, buttons, links, and accent elements

Dark Background (#2b2b2b)

Used for: Main background and footer

Medium Gray (#4C4C4C)

Used for: Section backgrounds and cards

Light Gray (#666666)

Used for: Borders and secondary elements

Typography

The font selection focuses on readability and modern appearance:

Montserrat Bold

Usage: Headings, titles, and important elements
Reason: Modern, clean, and highly readable for emphasis

Montserrat Regular

Usage: Body text, paragraphs, and general content
Reason: Excellent readability for long-form content
